Administration Panel

Welcome, Administrator!

In this special page you're able to perform CRUD (Create, Read, Update and Update) operations over your users, orders and items.
So if you want to add a new item to the main page (where users can buy your products) you can do it here.
Every change you make here, will modify the registers of the database. So be cautious about what you delete or update.
Don't worry about missclicks; for every change you attempt to do, a confirmation popup will appear on the screen.
